Admissions & Enrollment

Start the journey to a brighter future for your child. Follow our simple, four-step admissions process.

The 4-Step Enrollment Process

1

Submit Application

Complete the online application form and pay the non-refundable application fee.

2

Assessment & Interview

The student completes a readiness assessment and an interview with the Head of Admissions.

3

Receive Acceptance

Upon review, an official acceptance letter and enrollment packet are issued.

4

Final Registration

Submit all required documents and pay the initial term fees to finalize registration.

Download Application Form (PDF)

Required Documents Checklist

To successfully finalize registration (Step 4), please ensure you have the following ready:

  • Student's Birth Certificate (Original and Copy)
  • Two passport-sized photographs of the student
  • Copy of Parent/Guardian ID (Passport or Driver's License)
  • Previous School Report Card/Transcript (If applicable)
  • Health Record/Immunization Card

Frequently Asked Questions

What age must my child be to enroll in Kindergarten? +

For our Kindergarten program, students must be 4 years old by September 1st of the academic year they are applying for.

Is Financial Aid or Scholarship available? +

We offer a limited number of need-based scholarships and merit-based tuition discounts. Details and application forms are available upon request from the Admissions Office.

Does the school follow the national curriculum? +

Yes, our secular curriculum follows the national standard, but it is integrated with comprehensive Islamic studies, including Qur'an memorization, Fiqh, and Adab.
