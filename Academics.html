Our Academic Excellence

A curriculum designed to cultivate knowledgeable, ethical, and successful students prepared for the future.

The Integrated Curriculum

At Rashidiyah, our curriculum seamlessly blends **secular academic excellence** with **comprehensive Islamic education**. We ensure students meet national academic standards while establishing a deep understanding of the Qur'an, Sunnah, and Islamic manners (Adab).

Our goal is to produce graduates who are not only intellectually sharp but also possess strong moral character.

Key Subject Areas

Core Academics

  • Mathematics & Science
  • English Language Arts (Reading, Writing)
  • Social Studies & History
  • Computer Science & Technology

Islamic Studies

  • Qur'an Memorization (Hifz)
  • Fiqh (Islamic Jurisprudence)
  • Hadith and Seerah (Prophet's Biography)
  • Tawheed and Aqidah (Belief)

Character Development

  • Ethics and Islamic Manners (Adab)
  • Leadership Training
  • Community Service Projects
  • Critical Thinking Workshops
